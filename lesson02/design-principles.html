Lesson 02 - W02 - Design Principles

Andrea Blake

Visual Hierarchy

Netflix

netflix.com

The design of this website demonstrates visual hierarchy by the way the text goes from large to medium to small, directing the gaze of the viewer.

Rule of Thirds

Adamas

adamas.premiumcoding.com

The design of this website uses rule of thirds by the placement of the photos. The rings at the top land in the middle of the first and second columns, while the ring on the finger in the second image lands on the line between the second and third columns.

White Space and Clean Design

Build In Amsterdam

buildinamsterdam.com

This website demonstrates organized white space and clean design by the way it separates its images with white columns and rows. It's compact, sleek, and clean in its appearance.
