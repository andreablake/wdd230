Chamber of Highland City

Target Audience

The target audience for the Chamber of Highland City is business owners, entrepreneurs, and individuals wanting to engage, support, and have an impact within their local community.

Personas

Walter

Walter is a recently graduated college student with a degree in Entrepreneurship from Brigham Young University – Idaho. He is single, though open to dating to find the perfect wife.

In his free time, he’s meeting with other like-minded individuals, either at the park or at his favorite café, to discuss economic stability and increasing job opportunities to help those in need of work.

Being creative and wanting to find ways to solve problems, he’s interested in engaging with the community to see what it might be lacking. Creating and starting up a business to addresses these needs makes him feel like he is making a difference in his community.

Cindy

Cindy is a mother in her mid-thirties with two young teenagers. Her husband is a construction worker, and she is employed as a hiring manager for a software development company.

She is very practical and finds great satisfaction in helping people find the best jobs that suit them, their skills, and their personality. Everything must have its place, and that includes potential employees.

Because she wants to find the best people for the job, she’s interested in connecting with the local community to post job offers, network with other businesses, and observe what the employment status and job opportunities are across the city.

Scenarios

  • I want to learn more about this community and the locals.
  • Where can I check for business events so I can network with others?
  • What kind of businesses are supported in this area?
  • What are the current needs in my community?
  • How can I help support the community and get involved?
